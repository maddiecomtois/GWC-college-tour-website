The Problem

We did most of the heavy lifting for you to provide a default stylings that incorporate our custom components. Additionally, we refined animations and transitions to provide a smoother experience for developers.

Our Solution

We are planning to improve the process of scheduling and booking college tours by putting all the necessary information in one place: our website. Our site will collect all the user’s information when she or he signs up that would be needed for booking a college tour. This way students don’t have to re-enter their information every time they sign up for a tour, like they would normally do when booking on individual colleges’ websites. Our website will also allow users to search for colleges based on location, making it easier to book multiple college tours, or allow the users to search by college name. When the user searches the map, markers representing different colleges will pop up onto the screen. When the user clicks on a marker, a brief description of the college shows up on the side of the map, along with basic statistics and a calendar with available tours and times. This feature will give users the ability to schedule college tours with colleges that they hadn’t planned on visiting prior to visiting our website.
